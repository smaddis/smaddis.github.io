It’s a field day

Get hands-on with your research with our Smart and mobile testbed for Automated and assisted Driving. Test your applications and ideas in a real-life environment.

For Research

The car will function as an advanced research, development and testing environment for intelligent automotive systems and Cooperative Intelligent Transportation Systems. These systems co-operate fluently with their operational environment.

For Businesses

The car will also be available for use in product development and research. It will function as an internationally advanced field testing environment of automated and autonomously moving systems.

Research topics

  • Automotive software engineering
  • Intelligent systems
  • 5G / 6G technology
  • Optoelectronics and measurement technology
  • Sensors and energy harvesters
  • Machine vision research
  • Big data analytics
  • Human sciences (user experience, virtual learning, etc.)
  • Work machines and vehicles
  • Automated control based on data modeling in the construction and maintenance of fairways and driving dynamics and road safety research
